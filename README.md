# Privacy Policy – EUC SOH ANALYZER

_Last updated: 2026-03-31_

This privacy policy describes how **EUC SOH ANALYZER** handles data.  
The App is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

## Data collection

- The App does **not** collect any personal data.  
- The App does **not** collect device identifiers, location data, contact information, usage analytics, or any other user-related data.  
- The App does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Local processing and files

- The App may read data from files stored on the user’s device (for example, CSV files).  
- The App may generate files on the device (for example, PDF files).  
- All such processing happens locally on the device.  
- These files are stored only on the user’s device and are not transmitted to any external server by the App.

## User-initiated sharing

- The App may provide a “share” or “export” function that uses the operating system’s standard sharing mechanisms.  
- Any sharing of files or data is fully initiated and controlled by the user.  
- When the user chooses to share a file, the file is sent directly from the device to the third-party apps or services selected by the user (for example, email, messaging apps, cloud storage).  
- The App does not send any files automatically and does not receive any information about how or with whom the user shares those files.

## Children’s privacy

- The App does not collect any personal data from users, including children.  

## Data security

- Since the App does not collect or store personal data on external servers, the main security responsibility lies in how the user manages their device and the files created or shared from the App.  
- Users are responsible for choosing trusted apps and services when sharing files from the App.

## Changes to this privacy policy

- We may update this privacy policy from time to time.  
- Any changes will be reflected in this document, with an updated “Last updated” date at the top of the page.


## Third-party services

- The App does **not** integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or user tracking.  
- The App does **not** communicate with remote servers operated by the developer or by third parties.
